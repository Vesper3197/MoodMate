Our Mission

Combining chatbot technology with sentiment analysis can create intelligent counseling tools that can provide psychological support online 24/7. Additionally, it can also serve as an auxiliary tool for psychiatrists, improving the efficiency and quality of diagnosis and treatment, and widely improving the public's mental health level.
